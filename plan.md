## How We Communicated

We used WhatsApp as our main way of staying in touch — quick messages for updates, sharing screenshots when something worked or didn't. For longer working sessions we used Zoom so one of us could share their screen. We also caught up during tutorials each week which helped with the bigger tasks.

We agreed early on to message each other at least every few days and to let the other person know straight away if something wasn't working so we didn't waste time going in the wrong direction.

## GitHub Commits

We agreed that each of us commits after finishing a piece of work — whether that's writing a section, adding screenshots, or completing a practical task. We didn't want one person doing all the commits because that wouldn't reflect what actually happened. Commit messages describe what was actually done rather than just saying "update files."
